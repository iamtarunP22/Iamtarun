About Me

A LIFE OF
Freedom

Hello! I'm Tarun Kumar — an Indian traveler, writer, and free spirit. Born in the land of Bharat, I've fallen in love with every corner of this incredible country before exploring the world.

From the Himalayas to Kanyakumari, from Rajasthan's deserts to Kerala's backwaters — every road in India has a story. This portfolio is my way of sharing them.

My journey of freedom began the day I decided that experiences matter more than things. Since then, I haven't stopped moving.
